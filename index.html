Architecture That Builds Well

Our architects lead with site, climate, and program. We develop a clear kit of parts—structure, envelope, services— so contractors can price confidently and fabricators can shop drawings without guesswork.

Coastal resilience shapes our detailing: elevated slabs, continuous load paths, corrosion-aware assemblies, and shading strategies that reduce cooling loads from day one.

Accredited Testing Laboratories

Our testing group validates design assumptions with ASTM/ISO methods: blower-door and duct leakage, acoustic STC/NC, thermal imaging, and on-site envelope water intrusion (ASTM E1105).

Reports include raw data, calibrated equipment IDs, and pass/fail criteria matched to code or owner standards—ready for permitting officials, commissioning agents, and lenders.

Project Flow

Fewer surprises, better handoffs, predictable outcomes.

1Discovery & Feasibility. We map constraints—zoning, flood, utilities—and build a pro-forma aligned to scope, budget, and schedule.

2Design & Coordination. Iterative concepts, energy modeling, and BIM workshops with trades. Drawings drive bid clarity and permitting speed.

3Verification & Close-Out. Field testing validates envelope and systems; punchlists track to zero. Turnover includes O&M and testing reports.
