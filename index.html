Architecture That Builds Well

Site, climate, and program guide each concept. Structure, envelope, and services are organized into a clear kit of parts so trades can price and fabricate without guesswork.

Resilience directs detailing: elevated slabs, continuous load paths, corrosion-aware assemblies, and shading strategies that lower cooling demand from day one.

Accredited Testing Laboratories

We validate assumptions with recognized methods: blower-door and duct leakage, thermal imaging, envelope water intrusion and field acoustics.

Deliverables include raw data, calibrated equipment IDs, and acceptance criteria matched to code or owner standards—ready for officials, commissioning agents, and lenders.
